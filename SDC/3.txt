<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
    <head>
        <title>Page Title</title>
    </head> 
    <body>
        <h1>Welcome!</h1>
        <p>This is a paragraph.</p>
    </body>
</html>

<html>: Root element.
<head>: Contains meta information (like the title).
<body>: Contains visible content (text, images, etc.).

Common Tags:
<h1> to <h6>: Headings.
<p>: Paragraph.
<a>: Link.
<img>: Image.
<ul> / <ol>: Lists.
